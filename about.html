About Me

Hello and Welcome

I’m Sam, a front-end developer in training based in Exeter,UK. My journey into web development is driven by a passion for clean design, digital creativity, and building functional websites that people actually enjoy using. I have a solid understanding of HTML and CSS, and I’m now expanding into JavaScript to bring more dynamic, interactive features to my projects.

I believe in learning by doing. My portfolio already includes projects such as a Wikipedia-style page clone and a data collection form, which helped me practicestructure, styling, and responsive design principles. Each project sharpens my skills and brings me one step closer to professional-level development.

Alongside coding, I work part-time as a chef at Greene King, where I’ve learned the value of teamwork, time management, and staying composed under pressure—skills that carry over into my approach to development.

My journey is also deeply personal. I’m proud to be living in sobriety since June 2024, and I actively attend meetings and 12-step as part of my recovery. This discipline fuels my determination to keep improving, both as a developer and as a person.

Beyond work and coding, I enjoy digital art, gardening, and exploring creative design styles such as cyberpunk. As a father, I’m motivated to set an example of persistence, resilience, and ambition.

I’m building my future one line of code at a time—and I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and creating.
